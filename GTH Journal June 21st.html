June 21st

The sun was warm upon their napes and they found that they had ridden for many miles and were both parched by the time they came upon the stream. They drank from the same water as their horses. They drank heartily. Then they sat in the shade. Father grew tired. Their steeds also. So they rested. I love You said the Boy. Then I have all I need said Father.
